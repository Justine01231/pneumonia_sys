About

Learn about the pneumonia detection system

Model Used

Name: MediScan Pneumonia Detection v2.0

Architecture: ResNet-50 (Residual Neural Network)

Type: Deep Learning CNN (Convolutional Neural Network)

Framework: TensorFlow Lite

Training: Trained on thousands of chest X-ray images

The ResNet-50 model uses residual connections to analyze radiographic patterns and features that may indicate pneumonia infection in chest X-rays.

How It Works

  1. Enter patient information
  2. Upload chest X-ray image
  3. AI analyzes image patterns
  4. Receive diagnosis with confidence score
  5. Review Grad-CAM heatmap visualization

Grad-CAM Visualization

Grad-CAM (Gradient-weighted Class Activation Mapping) highlights the regions of the X-ray that most influenced the AI's decision.

Red/Yellow areas: High importance

Blue/Green areas: Lower importance

Privacy & Security

Patient data is stored securely in a local database. All information is encrypted and protected.

Images are processed locally and not sent to external servers.

Important Notice

This AI system is designed to assist medical professionals, not replace them. All results must be reviewed and verified by qualified healthcare providers.
